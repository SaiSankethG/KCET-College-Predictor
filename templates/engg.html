About

KCET College Predictor is an online tool that helps students predict their chances of getting admission into colleges based on their KCET (Karnataka Common Entrance Test) scores. By providing the required information such as score, category, and preferred courses, the tool generates a list of colleges that the student is likely to get admitted to. The predictions are based on past admission trends and data analysis, giving students a realistic idea of their college options. The tool is easy to use and can be accessed from anywhere, making it a convenient tool for students to plan their future academic pursuits.
